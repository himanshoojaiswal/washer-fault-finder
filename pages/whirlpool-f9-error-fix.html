Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find the error code?

The code typically appears on the digital display when the cycle stops. If you don't have a display, count the blinking lights.

Is it safe to fix it myself?

Most sensor and latch replacements are safe if you unplug the machine first. However, for electrical board repairs, consult a pro.
